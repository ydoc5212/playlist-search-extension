YouTube Playlist Search

Search every playlist you've ever made — right inside YouTube's Save dialog and your playlists library.

1

Grant access to youtube.com

So the extension can inject a search box into the Save dialog and your playlists page. It runs only on youtube.com, and nothing leaves your device.

2

Open your playlists library

Try the search bar at the top of your library — or save any video on YouTube to see it inside the Save dialog.

Where you'll see it

  • The Save to playlist dialog on any YouTube video
  • Your playlists library at youtube.com/feed/playlists
Tip: Search works for any playlist you own — even ones beyond YouTube's 200-playlist limit that the Save dialog can't load on its own.
